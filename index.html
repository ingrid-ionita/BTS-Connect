About BTS

BTS stands for Bangtan Sonyeondan or Bulletproof Boy Scouts. It is a 7-member band, formed of 3 rappers and 4 vocalists.
They debuted in 2013 with their first album '2 COOL 4 SKOOL', which featured classics such as "No More Dream" and "We Are Bulletproof Pt.2". They produce a majority of their music, co-written with the help of Pdogg and Slow Rabbit (their producers). In 2021, the CD single 'Butter' was released, which includes "Permission to Dance" and "Butter". World-renowned musician Ed Sheeran and British producer Steve Mac took part in making the song.
Bangtan Bombs and Run BTS! are popular series among the fans, which produced a lot of their funny moments. Check them out on YouTube!
